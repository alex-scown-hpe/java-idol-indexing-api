26      /**
27       * Specify one or more individual documents and / or a range of documents that you want to delete. You can use the 
28       * following formats to do this (if you want to combine the two formats you must separate them with plus symbols 
29       * with no spaces before or after a plus symbol):
30       * <dl>
31       *   <dt>doc IDs</dt>
32       *   <dd>Specify the IDs of one or more documents. If you want to specify multiple document IDs, you must separate 
33       *       them with plus symbols (there must be no space before or after a plus symbol).</dd>
34       *   <dt>range=[&lt;first doc&gt;,&lt;last doc&gt;]</dt>
35       *   <dd>Enter the document ID of the first and last document in a range of documents that you want to delete. You

37       * </dl>
38       * 
39       * @param docs The IDs of the documents to be deleted.
40       */

49      /**

53       * <p />
